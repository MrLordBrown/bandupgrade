  • Angry Sock Puppets

    Angry Sock Puppets is the culmination of twenty years of patient consideration, observant ruminations, and consistent aggrivation. Music is humanity's experiment to test our ability to convert emotions into action. ASP takes your hate and turns it into a lyrical weapon that disentegrates the ego. ASP takes your lust and cranks it into your lover's sin just the way they like it. ASP takes your hope and kisses it dandelions on the wind, convulsive allergic fits to those who would hold you down.
